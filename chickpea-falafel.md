ingredients:

  - 1 tbsp baking soda
  - 1 large onion, chopped
  - 2 dl chopped parsley
  - 4 garlic cloves
  - 1 tbsp salt
  - 2 tsp cumin
  - 1 tsp coriander powder
  - 1 tsp black pepper
  - 1 tsp seven spices
---

1. Soak the chickpeas for 18 hours
2. Combine ingredients and blend
